Features

On top of keeping your balances in one place BMB lets you graph your earnings over time and compare between sites to see which are most profitable. There's also a quick update button to get the latest balance without having to open the page yourself.

BMB is still in the early days of development so features are a bit limited but there's plenty more coming. Daily breakdowns, automatic balance updates and earnings alerts are just some of the features that I would like to implement. If these are the kind of things you're interested in then give some love to the project to speed me along.
